The problem

Gripping all day wears your hands out.

Your hands ache after a long day. They seize up on the last job. The numbness has been there for a while. Over years, this is what takes hands like yours out of the work too early.

Good tools and a good setup help your posture. They do not lower the grip force your hand repeats thousands of times a day.

WorkGrip does that. It takes part of the grip off your fingers and puts it on the glove.

Your feel stays the same

It does not dull your touch.

The glove carries part of the grip through the back of the hand, so your fingers close with less effort of their own. You keep your grip, your angle, and your control. You just use less of your own strength to hold the tool.
