The problem

A weak hand cannot squeeze hard enough.

Your fingers still move. They just cannot press hard enough anymore. You drop things. You cannot open a jar, turn a key, or hold a pen for long.

So you ask other people for help with small things. There is no good product for this. The powered ones cost thousands of dollars, look like medical equipment, and get discontinued.

Capstan adds the grip strength back, in a glove you would want to wear.

How it works

Three steps.

1

Put it on

It is a soft glove with a leather palm. Thin cables run along your fingers. You can put it on with one hand.

2

Turn the dial

A small dial at your wrist tightens the cables. You can set it by pressing it against a table. There is no battery to charge.

3

Grip

The glove holds your hand closed while you use it, then lets go. Open a jar, a bottle, a door, a spoon.
