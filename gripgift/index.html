You have noticed

You have thought about buying her a gadget. But the jar openers pile up in a drawer, and anything that looks like a medical aid feels like telling her she is getting old.

GripGift is different. It gives her grip back in something she would actually want to wear.

It is real leather and a dial like a watch. It sits on the dresser next to her good things, not in the drawer with the medical stuff. When she opens a jar in front of the grandkids without asking for help, that is the gift.

How it works

Simple enough that she will use it.

A soft leather glove that goes on easily, even with stiff, sore hands. One hand can do it.

2

A small dial at the wrist tightens it, pressed against the counter. No buttons, no charging, nothing to figure out.

3

She opens things

The glove holds her hand closed. The jar, the bottle, the key, the door. Then it lets go.
