Privacy policy

In plain English: what we collect, why, who we share it with, and your choices. We do not sell your personal information.

Who we are

This site is run by [Legal business name], contact hello@humankindbionics.com, [Business mailing address]. If you have any question about your data, email us and a real person answers.

What we collect

  • Your email address, when you join the list or reserve.
  • Your name and shipping or billing address, if you place a reservation.
  • Your reservation details (which product, which price).
  • Basic usage data collected automatically: your device type, rough location from your IP address, and which pages you viewed, through our analytics tool.

How payments are handled

Card payments are processed by Stripe. Your card details go to Stripe, not to us. We never see or store your full card number. Stripe has its own privacy policy at stripe.com/privacy.

Why we collect it

  • To hold your reservation and, later, to ship your order.
  • To answer your questions and give you support.
  • To email you about your reservation and shipping.
  • To prevent fraud and keep the site working.
  • To understand how the site is used, so we can improve it.
  • To send you marketing, only if you have opted in. You can opt out any time.

Who we share it with

We share data only with the services that help us run this: Stripe for payments, our analytics tool (Google Analytics or Plausible), our email tool, and our website host. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with advertisers.

Cookies and analytics

We use analytics to see how the site is used. This may set cookies. You can block cookies in your browser and still use the site. If you are in the EU or UK, we ask for your consent before using non-essential cookies.

Your choices and rights

You can ask us to show you your data, correct it, or delete it, and you can unsubscribe from emails at any time. Email hello@humankindbionics.com.

  • EU and UK (GDPR): you have the right to access, correct, delete, and port your data, to object to some uses, to withdraw consent, and to complain to your data protection authority.
  • California (CCPA/CPRA): you have the right to know what we collect, to delete it, to correct it, and to opt out of any sale or sharing. We do not sell or share your data, and we will not treat you differently for exercising your rights.

How long we keep it

We keep your reservation and contact details for as long as we need them to serve you, then delete them.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your data. No system is perfect, but we take this seriously.

Children

This site is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their data.
