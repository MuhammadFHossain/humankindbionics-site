The problem

A hard grip hurts the elbow.

The harder you grip, the more the elbow takes. Then tennis elbow sets in, the kind that takes a long time to heal, and your forearm cramps late in the match. You start skipping games. For most players, it is injury, not age, that ends the game.

Braces and straps treat the pain after it starts. They do not fix the cause, which is a hand that grips too hard because it has to.

TennisGrip holds part of the grip for you, so you keep a lighter grip and the elbow takes less.
