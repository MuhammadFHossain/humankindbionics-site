Our promise

Money-back guarantee

Two guarantees, in plain English. You are protected before your glove ships and after it arrives.

1. Before it ships: your deposit is fully refundable

Your $100 deposit is 100% refundable at any time before your order ships. No fees. No restocking charge. No forms to fill out. Change your mind for any reason, or no reason, and we return every cent.

You are not charged the full price today. Today you pay only the $100 refundable deposit to hold your place. We charge the rest only when your glove is ready to ship, and we email you first, so there is never a surprise charge.

2. After it arrives: 30-day money-back

When your glove arrives, try it for 30 days. If it does not help you, send it back in usable condition and we refund what you paid for the product. We cover return shipping.

How to get a refund

Email contact@farhanhossain.com from the address you used, or reply to your confirmation email. Tell us you want a refund. That is all we need.

How fast, and how

Refunds go back to your original payment method. We return deposits within 5 business days. If you paid by card, the credit appears within one billing cycle.

If we are late, or cannot ship

If our ship date moves, we email you a new date and you can cancel for a full refund at that point. If we decide not to build a product you reserved, we refund every deposit in full and tell you plainly. Nobody keeps your money.

The one exclusion

The 30-day return needs the glove to come back in usable condition. The $100 deposit itself has no conditions at all: it is always fully refundable before shipping.
