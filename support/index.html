Your deposit and reservation

What does my $100 deposit do?

It reserves your place in the first production batch and comes off your final price. It is not an extra charge. When your glove is ready, you pay the remaining balance, and the $100 is already counted toward it.

Is the deposit really refundable?

Yes. Fully refundable, any time, for any reason, by email or one email. There is no window and no restocking fee. If you change your mind, you get 100% of your deposit back.

What if you never ship the product?

Then we refund every deposit in full and tell you plainly. We are validating real demand before committing a production run. If a given product doesn't move forward, nobody keeps your money.

How do I get a refund?

Reply to your confirmation email, or write to hello@humankindbionics.com with the email you used. Refunds go back to your original payment method.

Shipping and timeline

When will it ship?

Where do you ship?

The first batch ships within the US to start. Join with your email and we'll tell you when we open to your country.

Will I be charged the full price now?

No. Today you place only the refundable $100 deposit. The balance is charged when your glove is ready to ship, and only after we've confirmed the date with you.

How it works and fit

How does the glove add grip with no battery?

It's fully passive. Thin, strong cables run along your fingers the way your own tendons do. A dial at the wrist tensions them, and the cables carry part of the grip force for you. There is nothing to charge, no app, and no electronics to fail.

How strong is the assist?

Enough that a hand which cannot break a jar seal or turn a stiff key can do both with the glove on. We are still measuring it properly across different hands, so we are not publishing a figure until that work is done and we can stand behind it.

Can I put it on by myself?

Yes. It's designed to go on with one hand, and the dial can be set by pressing against a table or your leg, so you don't need fine control in the other hand.

What sizes are there?

The first batch offers several sizes, and we'll help you fit yours before it ships. Hands vary, and getting the fit right is part of the first-batch process.

Is it a medical device?

No. It's a passive functional aid, like a well-made tool. It does not diagnose, treat, or cure anything, and it makes no medical claims.
